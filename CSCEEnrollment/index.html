Major Enrollments & Gender

This page provides some data on the enrollment figures for the last 10 years for both Computer Science and Computer Engineering. In addition, we have tracked the percentage of women in each major.

Data is based on numbers reported by the office of Insititutional Research & Planning (IRP) which is publicily available here.

  • Dashed lines are for Computer Engineering, solid for Computer Science
  • UNL Term Codes: the middle two digits are the year, the third is the month the term begins (1 = Spring, 8 = Fall), thus 1158 = Fall 2015
